FAQ

Who can participate?

Anyone currently pursuing Tertiary education.

I don’t have a team member. Can I still participate?

Teams must have only 2 members. You may email us at lifehack@nuscomputing.com and we can try to match you up with someone.

How will the submissions be assessed?

Submissions will be assessed by NUS Professors. Results will be released on 29th July. Selected finalist teams will be invited to present their solutions to judges on the Finale day on 30th July. Stay tuned until then!

Must I be present for the zoom session if my team is shortlisted?

At least one member of the team has to be present. If no members are available, the team will be disqualified.

Will COVID-19 affect the hackathon?

Since this is a fully virtual hackathon, it is not affected by COVID-19. The timeline will proceed as per normal.

Do I need any technical experience or knowledge?

Basic programming competencies are expected, else all are welcomed! We have problem statements and workshops ranging from basic to advanced levels.

My team has encountered a roadblock in our project, can I seek help?

Yes! We have a forum where our Holmusk partners and Professors will be on standby to answer all your queries. You can access the forum here.

Can I use code I previously wrote?

You are allowed to use previously written code. However, the copying of ideas and code is not allowed.

Are there any restrictions on platforms and languages which can be used?

No, feel free to use any programming languages or technology you’re comfortable with!
